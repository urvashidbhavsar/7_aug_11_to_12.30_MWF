traversing means move through any element in DOM tree.

in traversing,
(1) ancestor(parent) => parent(), parents(), parentsUntil()

(2) decendent(child) => children(), find()

(3) siblings(similar) => siblings(), next(), nextAll(), nextUntil(), prev(), prevAll(), prevUntil()

(4) filter(searching) => filter(), first(), last(), eq(), not()
